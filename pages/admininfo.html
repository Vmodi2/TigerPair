Easy sign-up

The sign-up process for Princeton students and alumni is a breeze. Students are authorized through Princeton's CAS login system and alumni are approved with a valid e-mail address on the alumni.princeton.edu domain.

Management features

Easily remove and re-pair participants in real-time within the dashboard. As participants sign up, new matches can easily be created without disrupting old ones!

Machine learning

As an administrator, you can easily choose the features you want to include, including keyword fields. And with an algorithm on the frontier of word embedding research in natural language processing, you can rest assured that computed matches between students and alumni will be optimal.

Importing features

If you'd rather avoid managing a sign-up process, TigerPair also offers easy-to-use tools that allow you to import student and alumni information from an Excel or Google Sheets spreadsheet or a Google Form. Match results can then easily be sent out to participants.
