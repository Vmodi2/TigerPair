Welcome to TigerPair

TigerPair is a user-friendly alumni engagement platform that finds optimal matches based on features chosen by the mentorship project administrator. Any organization can create an administrative account, setting a custom group name and ID for their members to join. Students and alumni can sign up themselves for any group, or have their profiles created by the admin. Using vectorization techniques, our matching algorithm can analyze keywords in the student and alumni profiles to find optimal pairs based on major, degree type, general interests, preferred degree of involvement, and any other feature that the admin desires to include.
